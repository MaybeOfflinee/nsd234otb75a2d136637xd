Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?
You can connect via Minecraft Java Edition (1.20+) using the address play.pinksmp.net. For the best experience, we recommend our custom launcher.
Can I play on Bedrock/Mobile?
Yes! Thanks to Geyser, both Java and Bedrock players can play together. The Bedrock port is 19132.
Are Ranks and Cosmetics permanent?
Yes. All Ranks and Cosmetics purchased from our store are Lifetime. You will keep them even after season resets.
Where can I get support?
Our support team is active 24/7 on our Discord server. Join us and open a ticket for any issues or questions.
